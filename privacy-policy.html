Privacy Policy for Drift

Last updated: 9 April 2026

Drift is a free, offline, single-player mobile game developed by Selim Celem. This policy explains what data the app does and does not handle.

Data we collect

None. Drift does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information, device identifiers, usage analytics, advertising IDs, or telemetry of any kind.

Data stored on your device

Drift stores your top 5 high scores locally on your device using the browser's localStorage API. This data never leaves your device. Uninstalling the app or clearing its storage removes it permanently.

Third-party services

Drift does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising networks, crash reporters, or cloud services. It does not require an internet connection to run. The app does load one web font (Google Fonts) on first launch if a connection is available; if no connection is available, a system fallback is used.

Permissions

Drift does not request any Android runtime permissions. It does not access your camera, microphone, location, contacts, storage, or any other sensitive APIs.

Children's privacy

Drift is safe for all ages. Because the app collects no data whatsoever, it is compliant with COPPA and similar regulations protecting children's privacy.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new "Last updated" date.
